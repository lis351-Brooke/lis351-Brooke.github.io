Crochet Wearables

Types of Crochet Clothing

Many people in the crochet community love making themselves clothing. This can be a little easier if you are making something for yourself rather than for someone else. Especially when starting out. Things that matter the most here are tension, hook size, and type of yarn. Some yarns are nicer to work with depending on the project. For example, if you are working on a winter hat, you do not want to be using the thinnest yarn you find, you would want one that is mostly wool and has a little bit of a thickness to it. However, if you are making something like a sweater or a cover up, a thinner yarn could be used here. What type of project you are trying to make matters a lot when choosing the type of yarn to use.
